FEATURES

Preserves Anonymity

Our software allows students with learning difficulties to remain anonymous to their notetaker. Nexchange eliminates the need for a third party to transfer notes from the notetaker to the student in need of notes.

Mobile-Friendly

The Nexchange platform is mobile-friendly, which allows students receiving notes and notetakers to upload or download notes without scanning them or transferring files to their computer.

User Analytics

The student accessibility center can easily analyze user activity using the administrator panel. This is useful to determine how much notetakers should be remunerated and to determine if students with learning difficulties are using the service.

Archive of Notes

The amount of notes increases every year, therefore creating an archive of notes. If a notetaker cannot be found for a given class where a student needs help, the accessibility center can grant access to a past iteration of a course.

Notifications

Students receiving notes can choose to be notified through email when new notes are uploaded. Notetakers will be notified if they have not posted notes within a certain amount of hours since their last class.

Organized

Students receiving notes can view their notes for each course on an organized home page. Sorting options allow them to find the notes that they are looking for with ease.
