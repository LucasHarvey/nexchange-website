Innovative

OUR STORY

Nexchange was created within the Sandbox, an entrepreneurship hub at John Abbott College. Wanting to make a difference in his school community, Lucas and his team started brainstorming about how technology can help students. What started as the idea for an online platform to exchange notes evolved into a fully developed product that makes a difference in the lives of students with academic difficulties.

Lucas Harvey

Founder & CEO

As a student that strives for the highest degree of excellence, Lucas wanted to combine his technological skills and academic distinction. Lucas leads Nexchange with the mindset that students should have access to customized educational tools that will lower their stress levels and make their journey through education as smooth as possible.
