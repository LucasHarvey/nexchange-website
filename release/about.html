OUR STORY

Nexchange was created within the Sandbox, an entrepreneurship hub at John Abbott College. What started as the idea for an online platform to exchange notes evolved into a fully developed product that makes a difference in the lives of students with learning difficulties. Nexchange has won both the local and regional levels of the OSEntreprendre competition and is now competing at the provincial stage.

Lucas Harvey
